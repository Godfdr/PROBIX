# PROBIX: Product & Brand Context

This document defines the brand lane and design vocabulary for the PROBIX terminal, initialized via the Impeccable skill.

## 1. The Brand Lane: "Technical Urushi"
PROBIX is a high-fidelity prediction terminal built for African expertise. It sits at the intersection of institutional-grade data and local market intuition.

*   **Audience:** Sophisticated African traders, analysts, and oracles.
*   **Voice:** Precise, authoritative, and fast.
*   **Visual Meta:** A crafted lacquer (Urushi) object with technical, circuit-like gold seams.

## 2. Design Vocabulary
These terms guide all future UI construction:

*   **Terminal Ground:** A deep lacquer surface (Lacquer Black).
*   **Oracle Seams:** Hairline-first depth using Kinpaku Gold.
*   **Pulse Status:** Verdigris Patina for active node synchronization and "live" market signals.
*   **Technical Geometry:** High-density data grids (Bento-style) with razor-sharp alignment.

## 3. Product Principles
1.  **Instant Navs First:** All navigations must feel immediate and pre-rendered.
2.  **Tactile Ticks:** Every price change or button press must provide a physics-based spring response.
3.  **African Precision:** Use tabular numbers and high-contrast typography to ensure readability in all light conditions.
